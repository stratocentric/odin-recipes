Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need for a split pea soup at home:

  1. Butter: Sauté the vegetables in butter for a rich flavor base.
  2. Vegetables: Split peas, carrots, celery and onions.
  3. Seasonings: This split pea soup with ham recipe is seasoned with fresh garlic, salt, pepper, and a bay leaf.

  5. Ham: A pound of diced ham lends tons of savory, meaty flavor.
  6. Stock: Use a quart of store-bought or homemade chicken stock (plus 2 ½ cups of water).

How to Make Split Pea and Ham Soup

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make split pea soup with ham at home:

  1. Cook the garlic, onions, and celery in butter.
  2. Add the split peas, ham, and bay leaf. Stir in the stock and water.
  3. Cook until the peas are tender and the soup is thick. Season with salt and pepper.
